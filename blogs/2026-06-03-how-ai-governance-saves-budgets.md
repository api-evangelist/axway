Enterprises face a critical challenge balancing widespread AI adoption with spiraling costs, as developer workflows consuming thousands of times more tokens than basic requests and continuous AI-powered chatbots drive token consumption to unsustainable levels. The real issue isn't AI itself but governance and cost management, requiring sophisticated controls including consumption quotas, per-model caps, usage monitoring, and semantic caching. Companies need to transform AI from unpredictable spending into a scalable, accountable tool that balances innovation with financial responsibility using platforms like Axway Amplify AI Gateway.
